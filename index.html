About Sunrise Academy

Founded in 1985, Sunrise Academy has been a beacon of educational excellence in our community for over four decades. We believe in nurturing not just academic brilliance, but the complete development of every child.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and innovative curriculum create an environment where students thrive. From kindergarten through 12th grade, we offer a transformative educational journey that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond.
