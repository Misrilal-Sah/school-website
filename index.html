Mr.John Anderson

Superintendent (1884-1914)

Mr Anderson was a born teacher of the old Scottish school. In his time, Bombay Scottish School was described as a school "in which the modern principles of education can never very adequately replace the individuality and personality of the teacher which counts so much in dealing with the young and is apt to be lost in the machinery of a codefied system".

Mrs.Molly Paul

Principal (2014-2019)

Mrs. Molly Paul is presently working as Alumnus of Bombay Scottish School, Mahim. Her love for children, led her to take up teaching as a profession and is a teacher at Bombay Scottish School, Mahim for 27 years. Held the post of Vice Principal at Bombay Scottish School, Mahim for 8 years. Taken charge as Principal of her Alma Mater in May 2014.

Mrs.Sunita George

Principal (2019)

Sunita George had been completed her Masters from University of Mumbai.she was Principal of R.N.Podar school earlier in 2013 .She was also Ex-Principal of VIBGYOR school(2014-16).Now she had joined Bombay Scottish School as a Principal in 2019.Also joined as a WES Speaker for 15th World Education Summit, to be held in Mumbai, 2019.
