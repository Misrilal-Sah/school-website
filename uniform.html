-------------UNIFORM-------------



Standards: Jr. KG to std. 10


Girls: Grey knee length tunic with blue sash, white half sleeved blouse, school tie with House badge, white socks and black sports shoes.


Boys: Grey shorts ( Stds. 9 & 10 must wear trousers), white half sleeved shirt, School tie with House badge, white socks and black sports shoes.


Students of the KG section only are permitted to wear black sports shoes with Velcro fastening.

P.T. Uniform: Stds. 1 to 10


Girls:T-Shirt in House colour, white P.T. skirt with piping in House colour, white socks and white sports shoes with white laces.


Boys: T-Shirt in House colour, grey shorts/trousers, white socks and white sports shoes with white laces.




Standards: 11 and 12


Girls:Blue and white checked half sleeved blouse, light blue knee length denim skirt, white socks and black sports shoes.


Boys: Blue and white checked half sleeved shirt and light blue denim trousers, plain black leather belt, white socks and black sport shoes.





Standards: 11 and 12


P.T. Uniform: T-Shirt in House colour, navy blue track pants and white sports shoes with white laces.
