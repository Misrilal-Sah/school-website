Sports

School sports refer to athletic programs in the context of the school setting. They refer most often to interschool competition at the middle/junior high school and high school levels.Sports like Football,Cricket ,Volleyball is played frequently.

Competition

Competition between students or teams of students within a classroom or across different schools regions are held.It helps develop problem-solving skills ,bring out skills, talents, gifts, and creativity that may not have seen within traditional classroom projects ,builds confidence.

Trips

School trips that involve adventure activities and sports can become the foundation of future interests in children. Such activities help children explore sports beyond phones and tablets thereby inducing physical activity that eventually helps them stay healthy.
